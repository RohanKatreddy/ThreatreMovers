NOTE: D+, D- for DMX, not XLR. 3 is D+, 2 is D-

connections:
(rs485) B to D- (DMX shield)
(rs485) A to D+ (DMX shield)
(rs485) DE, GND to GND (DMX shield)
(rs485) RE to pin 5 (slave arduino)
(rs485) R0 to pin 0 / RX (slave arduino)
(rs485) VCC to 5V (slave arduino)
(slave arduino) GND to GND (DMX shield)
